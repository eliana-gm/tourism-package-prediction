Tourism Package Purchase Prediction

This project leverages data science to predict whether a customer is likely to purchase a travel package, empowering tourism businesses with actionable insights for marketing and product development.

Executive Summary

  • Target: Predict binary outcome - Will the customer purchase the package?
  • Dataset: 20 features including demographics, behavior, and preferences
  • Goal: Boost marketing ROI by identifying potential buyers in advance

Business Insights

Exploratory data analysis revealed the following:

  • Married customers are less likely to convert than single/unmarried
  • Higher income and passport ownership strongly correlate with purchases
  • Basic and standard packages showed the highest conversion rates
  • Executives and younger customers tend to have higher conversion likelihood
